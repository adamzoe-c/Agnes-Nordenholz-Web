Slow Luxury Atelier

Defined by a deep respect for heritage and natural textures, Agnes Nordenholz crafts a narrative of "Slow Luxury." From the heart of Berlin, each creation honors the artisanal process, ensuring every stitch carries the soul of the maker.

Our Philosophy

Our mission is to create democratic designs possessing both functionality and beauty. Combining artisanal skill with a continuous drive to support emerging talent, Agnes Nordenholz has built a foundation where tradition meets the avant-garde.

A conversation with Anna Wendt, film producer

When I first met Anna, we immediately connected through our shared concern for our mother, who needed care. At that time, we were both intensely focused on organizing her care, confronted with dementia in all its facets, bureaucracy with health insurance companies and authorities, absurd everyday situations, and emotional exhaustion. We talked about responsibility and grief, guilt, and the struggle not only for our mothers but also for our own livelihoods.
