HELLO!

My name is Agnes, and I am the founder and designer of Agnes Nordenholz.

Born and raised in Tübingen, Germany, I moved to Vienna to train as a tailor and study fashion design at the University of Applied Arts Vienna under Marc Bohan, Helmut Lang, and Jean-Charles de Castelbajac.

"Nordenholz being my dear grandmother's maiden name... my great-grandfather worked as a merchant in the wool trade... another was a tanner."

After 14 years as co-founder and designer of the label Hartmann Nordenholz, I moved to Berlin to "return to my roots" and continue my work under the name AGNES NORDENHOLZ. One of my great-grandfathers was born in Berlin. He worked as a merchant in the wool trade for one of the largest European wool companies. Another was a tanner and later a leather goods dealer. My mother and grandmother always talked about them a lot, and I loved imagining their lives and work.

It is only natural that my favorite materials are wool, leather and lambskin, and that the sheep is the focus of my attention: for centuries it has provided the most natural and perfect materials and is the best choice for clothing that has a minimal impact on the environment.

Vision & Philosophy

The Label

AGNES NORDENHOLZ is a slow luxury label founded in Berlin in 2015. Agnes Schorer is the creative mind behind the brand, crafting products with "soul" that withstand the fast-paced society of our time.

Craftsmanship

Agnes has chosen traditional craftsmanship and high-quality materials, processed in Europe according to ethical and sustainable guidelines, resulting in a product with true value.

Slow Luxury

Agnes Nordenholz celebrates the slowness of a design process in order to create products that retain their value and relevance for a lifetime.
